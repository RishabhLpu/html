Simple Omelette Recipe

Enjoy a quick and easy dish that's perfect for any meal. This classic omelette combines beaten eggs cooked to perfection, with optional fillings like cheese, vegetables, or meats.

Preparation Time:

  • Total: Approximately 10 minutes
  • Preparation: 5 minutes
  • Cooking: 5 minutes

Instructions:

  • Beat eggs and season with salt and pepper.
  • Heat a non-stick pan with butter or oil.
  • Pour in the eggs and cook until edges start to set.
  • Optionally add cheese, vegetables, or meats.
  • Fold the omelette in half and serve hot.
